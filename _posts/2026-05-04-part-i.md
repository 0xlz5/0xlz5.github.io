## PART I — Preface & Temporal Drift Log (2011–2025)

### 0 — Preface: A Small Theory of Technical Memory

Every technical life has two timelines:

- the one we *live*  
- and the one we *accumulate*  

Bookmarks, half‑read papers, strange tools, forgotten repos, late‑night experiments —  
they don’t disappear. They settle.

Understanding doesn’t move linearly.  
It moves in spirals.

This document is a drift through those spirals:  
a constellation of fragments that only make sense when viewed together.

It’s not a timeline.  
It’s a **constellation**.

### 1 — Temporal Drift Log (2011–2025)  
*A quiet archive of things that never fully left*

2011–2025 is not just a span of time.  
It’s a layered sediment of:

- tools explored “just to see”  
- ideas that resurfaced years later  
- technologies that were niche and then became essential  
- curiosities that turned into patterns  

Some things age.  
Some things mature.  
Some things wait.

This log is for the things that waited.
