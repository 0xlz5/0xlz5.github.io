# Part F — Post‑Scriptum: On Time, Play & Technical Identity

If there’s a theme running through all these sections, it’s this:

> **Technical curiosity ages well.**

Not everything becomes useful.  
But everything leaves a trace.

Over‑engineering is not a flaw.  
It is a form of play.  
And play is how we learn things we didn’t know we needed.

The drift continues.
